About Us

Building Skills,
Shaping Futures

Thongphong Vetsaphong Vocational School is dedicated to providing high-quality technical and vocational education that equips students with practical skills for meaningful employment and community development.

"Our graduates are not just certified — they are job-ready, community-minded, and equipped with real-world skills that make a difference."
